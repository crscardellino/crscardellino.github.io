About me

When I started the university I thought of myself as a programmer. Like most of all, I've started with the idea of programming videogames. I'd also flirted with hacking and security. I like logic, and computability theory, and I still enjoy the pleasure of coding. For the last 4 years I've been a Python developer although I used C or C++ from time to time. Now, however, I've been diving in the sea of Scala, just for a change, and I've been abandoning other tools since then (although I still use JavaScript for front end development). I'm also a regular user of text processing languages, mainly the ones in BASH: AWK and SED being my top choice.
